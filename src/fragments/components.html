Button

Lets people take an action with a single tap.

Stable

Use the primary button for the main action on a view; ghost for secondary actions; danger for destructive ones.

Four emphasis levels — pick the lowest that still reads as the right action.

    

      
Primary · high
The single main action on a view.

      
Secondary · medium
Supporting actions next to a primary.

      
Subtle · low
Tertiary or inline actions.

      
Danger
Destructive actions like delete.
